Ayuda Mutua
We are a collective group of Bushwick residents, long-term residents and recent arrivals, rooted in the mission of creating a local network for neighbors to support neighbors.

Sign up to volunteer

We are always looking for more neighbors to support our mission. From on-site food handling to connecting with folks in need of assistance, PR, awareness building, fundraising, finance, and tech, tell us about you and how you can help.

Get assistance

We offer food assistance and access to essential items like feminine products, soap, diapers, and baby food. We make referrals to organizations we trust can support you in legal support, housing assistance, medical assistance, and more.
