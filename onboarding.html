BAM
Onboarding Kit
Use the Google Translate toggle to read this in your preferred language
Bushwick Ayuda Mutua (BAM) is a volunteer-run mutual aid coalition that, via individual- and organization-level partner efforts, strives to maintain a solidarity economy in Bushwick. BAM provides access to food, essential goods, and social services via direct fundraising and partner groups.
While working together to build autonomous spaces for ourselves, regardless of the interpersonal dynamics that develop (note: you may make close friends with folks), we all occupy a figurative co-working space together, on a project whose scale takes considerable work to maintain.
Covered in this document are:
  • How we are structured,
  • How we communicate,
  • Our decision making strategies,
  • Discretionary spending protocols,
  • Legal and financial historical context,
  • A rough map of the projects & point people within BAM,
  • Brief notes on community resources,
  • Notes on volunteer expectations and conflict resolution, and
  • What equity looks like for those involved.
By participating as a volunteer with BAM, you agree to this operating document and to engage with our guiding principles in mind.
